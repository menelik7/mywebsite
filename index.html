About me

I am originally from Ethiopia and have been living in the U.S. for half my life. I currently work as a Project Engineer at BenchMark Management LLC, mainly managing vertical construction projects. My job is challenging and requires a good deal of time management. I enjoy what I do, but it does not provide opportunities to be genuinely creative.

I have been fascinated with software development dating back to the first time I was introduced to the Commodore 64. The owner's manual included pre-written programs. One rendered miniature hot-air ballons flying across the screen in random directions; and the other, was a segment of a musical keyboard. The rush I felt when the codes worked was indescribable. The fantasy of getting there is even more addictive. I have finally surrendered to my passion.

A reverse-phone lookup application that is quite user-friendly. There are no hoops to jump through; no unnecessary steps to complete. The user simply enters the phone number he/she wishes to lookup, and voila! Information specific to the user is dynamically displayed, as well as the option to lock-in on the associated address with the help of Google Maps API. The reverse phone is made possible by Whitepages API.

I worked on a team to develop this app, and I was responsible for the logic and database (Javascript & Firebase), AJAX method for API queries, as well as Firebase Authentication.
